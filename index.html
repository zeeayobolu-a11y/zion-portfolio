About Me

From Port-Harcourt
to the web.

Born in Port-Harcourt, Rivers State, and raised in Sagamu, Ogun State since April 2014. My secondary education was split between Divine Life College (JSS1-3) and Orion Academy (SS1-3), where I graduated with distinctions in Physics and Mathematics.

I started coding at 13, touching HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP. Today I work across web development, AI context engineering, and automation, building systems for real Nigerian businesses. How I Build

From brief to live site.

01

Client Brief

Onboarding call and Google Form to capture brand, goals, target audience, and content assets before a single line is written.

02

Prototype

Rapid visual prototype built on Lovable.dev. Client sees a real preview link within 24 hours, no waiting on wireframes.

03

Refinement

Site goes live on Netlify for preview. Client reviews on their actual domain via Hostinger after sign-off. Zero downtime handover.
